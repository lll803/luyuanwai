About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your mission is to survive against relentless waves of monsters. As time passes, the challenge increases with stronger enemies and more chaotic battles.

The game combines elements of roguelike progression with arcade-style action. Each run is unique, allowing you to discover new strategies and build different character abilities as you play.

With its captivating graphics and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for gamers of all skill levels.

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Test your survival skills against never-ending waves of monsters that get progressively more challenging.

Character Progression

Level up your character during each run, unlocking new abilities and enhancing your combat effectiveness.

Diverse Enemies

Face a wide variety of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns that require different strategies.

Power-ups & Items

Collect various power-ups and items throughout the game to enhance your abilities and increase your chance of survival.

Survival Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Prioritize leveling up movement speed and attack rate early in your run
  • Focus on collecting experience orbs from defeated enemies
  • Try to maintain distance from larger groups of enemies
  • Don't neglect defensive upgrades - survival is the ultimate goal
  • Experiment with different character builds to find what works best for your playstyle
